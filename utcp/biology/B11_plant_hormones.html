Plant Hormnoes: Auxins, Gibberelins & Ethene

Auxins are a class of plant hormones, with morphogen-type charicteristics, that play a role in the control of many growth & behavioural processes.
Fun fact! They derive their name from the Greek word αυξειν (auxein – "to grow or increase")
		    
Gibberelins are plant hormones that regulate developmental processes such as stem elongation, germination, flowering and more. Gibberelins are one of the longest-known classes of plant hormones.

Ethylene (ethene) is a naturally occuring gas, with the formula C2H6, which also acts as a plant hormone that controls ripening of fruit, the opening of flowers and other similar processes.
