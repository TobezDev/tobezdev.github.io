Privacy Policy for To-Do Bot

Introduction

Welcome to To-Do Bot! This document outlines our approach to user privacy and the information collected by our Discord bot.

Data Collection

To-Do Bot collects and processes the following data:

  • User Data: To-Do Bot may collect user data, including Discord user IDs, for the purpose of providing task management functionality.
  • Task Data: Information about tasks created by users, including task descriptions and IDs, is stored for the sole purpose of delivering the intended service.

Data Usage

To-Do Bot uses collected data for the following purposes:

  • Task Management: User and task data are used to facilitate task creation, retrieval, and removal within Discord.

Data Security

To-Do Bot is committed to protecting user data. We employ industry-standard security measures to safeguard the information we collect.

All task data is removed completely from our systems when you remove the task from your to-do list.

All user data is removed completely from our systems when you delete your account via email or via Discord.

Third-Party Access

To-Do Bot does not share user data with third parties outside of Discord.

- To-Do Bot reserves the right to share any data with Discord if it is deemed to break their Terms of Service or is illegal in any country.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy, please contact us via email.
